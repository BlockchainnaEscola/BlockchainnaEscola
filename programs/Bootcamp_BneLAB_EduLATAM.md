# Blockchain na Escola (BnE) Lab - Bootcamp Results
**October 21-28, 2025 | Belo Horizonte, Minas Gerais, Brazil**

---

## üéì Overview

From October 21-28, 2025, Blockchain na Escola (BnE) delivered a comprehensive 4-day Web3 bootcamp to 35 public high school students from two state schools in Belo Horizonte: **Escola Estadual Geraldo Jardim Linhares** and **Escola Estadual Cristiano Machado**.

This program represents a pioneering effort in blockchain education for Brazilian public schools, designed to democratize access to emerging technology knowledge and create pathways to Web3 careers for underserved communities.

**Every outcome is verifiable on-chain.** All student activities‚Äîfrom wallet creation to smart contract deployment‚Äîare permanently recorded on Celo Mainnet and Avalanche C-Chain, providing transparent, immutable proof of educational impact.

---

## üìä Program Impact at a Glance

- **35 students** onboarded to Web3
- **174 on-chain transactions** across Celo Mainnet
- **5 career tracks** presented by industry professionals
- **100% transaction success rate**
- **Zero passive recipients** - every student engaged in 3+ blockchain activities
- **1:0.8 catalyst ratio** - for every distribution transaction, students generated 0.8 additional autonomous transactions

## üåç About Blockchain na Escola

Blockchain na Escola (BnE) is a pioneering Brazilian educational initiative founded in 2022, dedicated to bringing blockchain education to public schools across Brazil. Our mission is to democratize access to emerging technology knowledge and create pathways to Web3 careers for underserved communities.

Since 2022, we have delivered free blockchain education programs in Salvador, S√£o Paulo, Rio de Janeiro, and Belo Horizonte, reaching nearly 1,000 students from public schools.

Our work is grounded in the belief that **education is a public good** and that access to knowledge about emerging technologies should not be determined by socioeconomic status.
